Workshop on Building Plan according to
'Vastu Shastra'

9/11/19 to 10/11/19

Topic Overview:
Vastu shastra is a traditional Indian system of architecture originating in India which literally translates to "science of architecture." These are texts found on the Indian subcontinent that describe principles of design, layout, measurements, ground preparation, space arrangement, and spatial geometry. Vastu Shastra incorporate traditional Hindu and in some cases Buddhist beliefs. The designs are intended to integrate architecture with nature, the relative functions of various parts of the structure, and ancient beliefs utilizing geometric patterns, symmetry, and directional alignments.
Workshop Detail:
1)VASTU SHASTRA
2)Sandard Size of The Architecht PLAN DOOR & WINDOW DETAILING
3)STAIR DETAILING
4) PLAN 1 BHK, 2 BHK, 3 BHK
5)FOUNDATION
