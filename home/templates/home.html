CivilERA at a Glance

The CivilERA was established with a prime objective to proliferate knowledge & address industrial issues by bringing proffesional needs, requisite training and students on a common platform. CivilERA, having a large force of students as it's member and faculties always ready to provide the mentorship, is one of the most active organizations of Haldia institute of technology.
We aim to promote Civil Engineering by providing them much-needed practical exposure through its regular activities like technical seminars, research symposium,Worshops,techtalks,site visit etc throughout the year and many other activities for enrichment of there skills. Collaboration between the students and industrial requirements is important part of building the advanced and skillful budding proffesionals.With this aim, to give our students some practical insight into industrial needs, we try to organize several possible visit throughout the year to ongoing construction sites thus giving them a chance to interact with key personnel of the industry.
The CivilERA also aims for publications of annual magazine which would help students in students for highlighting and going through the latest innovations. Apart from the technical aspects, it also undertakes the responsibility of proper nurturing of students by organizing events as to the break the ice for newcomers. Round the clock we are ready to transform the students as per industrial demand and demand of curriculum too.
